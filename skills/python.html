Python.

Honestly, I never thought I would have a passion for programming. I wanted to be a math teacher growing up, but I brushed that aside when I decided to pursue journalism as a major. But, I felt like there was something missing during my first semester at UT. When I found out about data journalism, it sounded like the perfect combination of the technical and creative parts of myself. My boyfriend, a computer science graduate, encouraged me to delve in the field. So, I took my first computer science class in the spring of 2020 at UT where we focused on Python, the first programming language I have learned. I continued my studies during the summer with a class on software design. Currently, I am taking data analytics and software engineering I courses at UT.
