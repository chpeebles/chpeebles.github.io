The Daily Texan: Double Coverage.

The Double Coverage issues of The Daily Texan were produced to provide readers with previews of UT football games throughout the fall 2020 season. These issues were published a day or so in advance of games and included stories from The Daily Texan sports staff, such as "Games to Watch" and "Big 12 Power Rankings." As the Double Coverage Design Editor, I designed a style guide and title graphics to create brand consistency throughout each individual issue. To assist with the page layout design of these 12 to 14-page issues each week, I trained and managed a team of two designers.

By the numbers:

We produced 10 flipbooks throughout the semester (some games & corresponding issues were canceled due to COVID-19). In total, I designed 52 pages, including the cover and feature story spreads I designed for every issue.
