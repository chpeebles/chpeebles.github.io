About Me.

designer, developer, learner

I am a senior at the University of Texas at Austin studying journalism and pursuing an elements of computing certificate. I currently intern with the marketing department at Proximity Learning and work as the Design Editor at the Daily Texan where I have been since the fall of 2019.

As a journalism student, I've learned how ask people about the problems they face. As a designer, I've learned how to use the experiences of others to inform my creations. As a developer, I aim to build sustainable solutions. In my work, I value inclusivity, empathy, and growth.

In my spare time, I like to try new recipes, play games, rock climb, and explore new places with my boyfriend, Zach!
